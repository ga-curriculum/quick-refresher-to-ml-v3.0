# Quick Refresher for ML

## About

This session provides a quick refresher on machine learning, focusing on the types of ML, commonly used algorithms, and practical applications. By the end of this session, you will have a solid conceptual understanding of machine learning approaches and be prepared to engage with advanced AI topics throughout the course. You will also be able to participate in technical discussions about machine learning models with clarity by:

- **Identifying the types of machine learning**: Supervised, Unsupervised, and Reinforcement Learning.
- **Describing common machine learning algorithms**: Understanding their purpose and why they are commonly used.
- **Applying ML concepts**: Classifying business problems based on the appropriate ML approach and reasoning through algorithm selection.
- **Recognizing practical challenges**: Understanding limitations and risks like bias, overfitting, and data quality issues.
